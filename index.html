Meet Aapo…

I'm a Cloud Solution Architect who believes accessibility isn't a checkbox — it's an engineering discipline.

I work with enterprise and public sector customers on Azure architecture, application modernization, and developer enablement. But what drives me most is building inclusive technology — making sure what we build works for everyone.

I lead accessibility initiatives across Europe North via Microsoft's GCAP community, and I organize major accessibility events like Saavuta and Esteet Nurin (accessible gaming!).

I'm also a builder at heart — I shipped a narrative game on Steam called "After Life", built it from scratch in ~11 months. Whether it's cloud infrastructure or game logic, I like getting my hands dirty.
