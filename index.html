About

I'm a Cloud Solution Architect based in Espoo, Finland, working with enterprise and public sector customers on Azure architecture and modern engineering practices.

A major theme in my work is accessibility as an engineering and leadership discipline — bringing accessibility into strategy, architecture, and delivery instead of treating it as a late-stage checkbox.

I lead and contribute to accessibility initiatives across Europe North via Microsoft's GCAP community, and I've organized and delivered major accessibility events, including Saavuta 2025 and Esteet Nurin 2025.

On the engineering side, I design and review cloud-native architectures, modernize applications, and enable teams with infrastructure-as-code and DevOps practices.

I'm also a builder: I have a background in game development and have released a game on Steam ("After Life"), built from scratch over ~11 months, including scripting, prototyping, and integrating audio/voice work.
